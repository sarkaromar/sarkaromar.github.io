Book Search

Asian University of Bangladesh (AUB) Library, established in 1996, is the heart of the university. The university library offers it's users a full range of information and enquiry services with the help of state-of-the-art techniques and technologies. The Library's mission is to enable and enrich the teaching, learning and research endeavors of the University by delivering flexible access to relevant information resources and by providing quality services and facilities. AUB has 4 medium sized libraries in its 4 campuses located at 1. Uttara, Dhaka 2. Dhanmondi, Dhaka 3. Rajshahi and 4 Khulna containing of about 96000 books, 1350 volumes of journals, 350 audio-visual materials.

The vision of AUB library is to offer a strong partnership with the University's teaching and learning community to enable a more direct contribution to the educational mission of the Library

The AUB library is now partially automated through flexible software, developed by the Department of Computer Science and Engineering of the university.

Library Timing

Sunday to Friday 9.00 am to 5.00 pm (Without any break) Saturday Closed

Library Location

AUB Library is located on the 4th floor of the Academic Building No.1.

Circulation Section: The Circulation section is located on the 4th floor of the Academic Building No.1. Lending and borrowing services, users' guidance, reference services, referral services, in-house telephonic response and any query regarding Library services are maintained by Circulation Section. A team consisting of young and dynamic library professionals work for the Circulation Section.

Library Committee

The Library is advised by a committee consisting of representatives from different faculties and departments of the University. The Vice Chancellor of Asian University of Bangladesh acts as the Chairperson of the Library Committee. On the other hand, the Librarian of AUB Library acts as the Member-Secretary of the Committee.

Responsibilities of the Committee are to advise the Library personnel of AUB Library in formulating library policies, procuring library materials, improving library and information services, operational matters, and so on.

Library Collection

The Library collection has grown steadily. At present, the total number of library books, journals and audiovisuals are 96000 and 1350 and 350 respectively.

The Collection of AUB Library comprises of books and periodicals related to the field of business studies, economics, liberal arts, social sciences, electrical and electronic engineering, physics, pharmacy, computer science, mathematics, English language and literature and Bangladesh studies.

The Library also stocks reference publications such as atlases, dictionaries, encyclopedias, The Banglapedia, annual reports and publications, survey reports, census reports, country reports, statistical yearbooks and other publications

Library Objectives

To support the mission of the University for emerging as a premier academic university, embracing traditional values of higher education as well as the knowledge required for the global community in the 21 st century.

To identify, acquire, organize, store, and provide on-demand access to the available intellectual and research products of scholars and to support teaching, learning, research and creative endeavors.

To provide increasing access to digital collections while continuing to build and improve access to collections in all formats to meet the research and teaching needs of the university.

To foster information literacy by working with the AUB community to think critically, create new knowledge, and be life-long learners.

Library Administration and Personnel

Library Administration :
The overall library administration is the responsibility of the Librarian, Asian University of Bangladesh (AUB).

Library Personnel :
To meet the growing demand of users, professionally trained personnel are responsible for taking care of the AUB Library and its contents, including the selection, processing, and organization of materials and the delivery of information, instructions, and loan services to meet needs of its users.
